Memento

Memento is a 2000 American mystery thriller film written and directed by Christopher Nolan. It's about a man who suffers from short-term memory loss and the inability to form new memories. He is searching for the people who attacked him and killed his wife, using an intricate system of Polaroid photographs and tattoos to track information he cannot remember. Since I am a big fan of the mystery/thriller/psychological genre, this movie easily became a favorite especially since it came from one of the best directors in my opinion Christopher Nolan.
