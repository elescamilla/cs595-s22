Strangers From Hell

Strangers From Hell is a South Korean television series that is streaming on Netflix. It's about a young man in his 20s who moves to Seoul after landing an internship in a company. While looking for a place to stay, he stumbles upon Eden Studio, an ominous cheap dormitory and decides to stay there as he is low on money. As time goes on, mysterious occurrences start occurring in the studio, causing the main character to start fearing the studio's residents. This series is easily one of my favorites as it sets the atmosphere very well and makes the audience feel like they're in the main character's situation.
