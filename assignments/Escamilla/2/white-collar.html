White Collar

White Collar is your typical crime fighting show with one big twist. Neil Caffrey, a notorious art thief and expert forger, is an is now working as a criminal informant for FBI Agent Peter Burk, the man who put Caffrey behind bars. Each episode is self-contained so it's easy to watch with friends even if they haven't seen every episode. But, there is also an interesting storyline that weaves through the entire series.
