Great British Bake Off

Great British Bake Off is the perfect mix of comedy and competition in a light-hearted baking competition. Home bakers create professional quality desserts in 3 rounds: Signature, Technical, and Show-Stopper. Each week, 1 baker is awarded "Star Baker" and 1 baker is sent home until the group is down to 3 bakers. The Star Baker of the final is crowned that season's winner.
